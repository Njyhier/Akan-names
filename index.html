Akan Names

The Ghanaian people have a very unique naming culture where a baby is given a name that corresponds to the day they are born. There are names that are reserved for males and others for the females. Do you know your Akan name? Well, here are the names:

Male Names

  1. Sunday: Kwasi
  2. Monday: Kwadwo
  3. Tuesday: Kwabena
  4. Wednesday: Kwaku
  5. Thursday: Yaw
  6. Friday: Kofi
  7. Saturday: Kwame

Female Names

  1. Sunday: Akosua
  2. Monday: Adwoa
  3. Tuesday: Abenaa
  4. Wednesday: Akua
  5. Thursday: Yaa
  6. Friday: Afua
  7. Saturday: Ama

About

This application calculates a person's day of birth and returns their Akan name according to their gender.
